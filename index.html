About Seiryo Znurt

Seiryo Znurt (or UZnurt, UTAUZnurt) is an Utauloid with a clear, highpitched voice and a modern design. Their existence is a work-in-progress, and their initial voicebank is a strong foundation for creators to use. The character's core concept is open to interpretation and designed to be a canvas for others. We hope you enjoy using them in your projects and look forward to hearing what you create.
